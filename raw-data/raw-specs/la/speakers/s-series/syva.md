**Short description**

2-way passive enclosure with six 5 in drivers and three 1.75 in diaphragm compression drivers coupled to a waveguide to achieve a coherent wavefront with a 140° horizontal by 26° vertical coverage.

**Description**

2-way passive enclosure with six 5 in drivers and three 1.75 in diaphragm compression drivers coupled to a cylindrical waveguide with 140° of horizontal directivity and a tightly controlled 26° of vertical directivity. The enclosure cabinet is constructed of premium grade Baltic beech and birch plywood with a slim elegance for easy integration. The enclosure front is protected by a coated steel grill and an acoustically transparent fabric. The enclosure integrates rigging and safety inserts for flown, wall-mount, pole-mount and stacked configuration. The enclosure integrates a signal connector on the bottom panel to connect to the companion subwoofer. Exclusively driven and protected by a proprietary amplified controller through a dedicated preset.
