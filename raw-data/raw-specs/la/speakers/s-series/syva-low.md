# Syva Low (L-Acoustics, S Series)

## 출처 1: Syva_Low_AE_EN.docx (동일 폴더에 원본 보관)

- Product line: S Series
- 2 x 12' Low-end extension

**Short description**

Passive low-end extension featuring two 12 in speakers with high-excursion drivers and optimized laminar vents to maximize LF efficiency - deployed in omnidirectional patterns in stacked configurations.

**Description**

Passive low-end extension with two high-excursion, direct-radiating 12 in transducers mounted in a bass-reflex tuned enclosure with laminar vents for increased LF efficiency. The enclosure cabinet is constructed of premium grade Baltic beech and birch plywood. The enclosure front is protected by a coated steel grill and an acoustically transparent fabric. The enclosure integrates a signal connector on the top panel to connect to the companion full-range enclosure. Exclusively driven and protected by a proprietary amplified controller through a dedicated preset.

**Technical requirements**

Coverage:

- Enclosure directivity: standard

Transducers:

- LF transducer: 2 × 12" cone drivers

Acoustics:

- Nominal impedance: 4 Ω
- Usable bandwidth: 40 Hz - 130 Hz (-10 dB), 42 Hz - 110 Hz (-6 dB), 47 Hz - 93 Hz (-3 dB)
- RMS power handling (Calculated using the mean impedance measured on the usable bandwidth): 894 W
- Maximum SPL: 137 dB Peak level at 1 m under half space conditions using pink noise with crest factor 4
