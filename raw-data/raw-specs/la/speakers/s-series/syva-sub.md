**Short description**

Passive subwoofer featuring one 12 in speakers with high-excursion driver and optimized laminar vents to maximize LF efficiency - deployed in omnidirectional patterns in stacked configurations.

**Description**

Passive subwoofer with a high-excursion, direct-radiating 12 in transducer mounted in a bass-reflex tuned enclosure with laminar vents for increased LF efficiency. The enclosure cabinet is constructed of premium grade Baltic beech and birch plywood. The enclosure front is protected by a coated steel grill and an acoustically transparent fabric. The enclosure integrates a signal connector on the top panel to connect to the companion full-range enclosure. Exclusively driven and protected by a proprietary amplified controller through a dedicated preset.
