# Kara II (L-Acoustics, K Series)

## 출처 1: Kara_II_AE_EN.docx (동일 폴더에 원본 보관)

- Product line: Kara II - Long throw line source, K Series
- Long throw > 35 m, deployment in vertical lines, incremental coverage with variable inter-element angles

**Short description**
2-way active enclosure with two 8 in drivers and one 3 in diaphragm compression driver - 10-degree enclosure angle with adjustable waveguide directivity for assembling enclosures to achieve a coherent wavefront line source.

**Description**
2-way active enclosure with two weather-resistant premium 8 in transducers and high-output 3 in diaphragm compression driver coupled to a cylindrical waveguide with user-adjustable horizontal directivity and 10° of vertical coverage. The enclosure cabinet is constructed of premium grade Baltic beech and birch plywood. The enclosure front is protected by a coated steel grill and an acoustically transparent fabric. The enclosure integrates four ergonomic handles. A four-point captive hardware for rigging and combining with other loudspeakers of the same family to achieve a coherent wavefront line source. The line source directivity depends upon the number of elements and inter-element angles and can be matched to the audience geometry. Exclusively driven and protected by a proprietary amplified controller through a dedicated preset.

**Technical requirements**

- Coverage (-6 dB):
  - Enclosure directivity: dependent upon the number of elements and the line source curvature
  - Waveguide directivity: 70° / 110° symmetric or 90° asymmetric (35°/90°)
- Transducers:
  - LF transducer: 2 x 8" cone drivers
  - HF transducer: 3" diaphragm compression driver
- Acoustics:
  - Nominal impedance: 8 Ω LF, 8 Ω HF
  - Usable bandwidth: 55 Hz - 20 kHz (-10 dB), 63 Hz - 20 kHz (-6 dB), 80 Hz - 19 kHz (-3 dB)
  - RMS power handling (calculated using the mean impedance measured on the usable bandwidth): LF 280 W / HF 75 W
  - Maximum SPL: 142 dB (peak level measured at 1 m under free field conditions using pink noise with crest factor 4)

**Physical data**

- Acoustical load: bass-reflex
- Cabinet: premium grade Baltic birch plywood
- Dimensions (W, H, D): 730 mm, 250 mm, 383 mm / 28.7 in, 9.8 in, 15.1 in
- Weight (net): 26 kg / 57 lb
- Connectors: 2 × 4-point speakON
- IP: IP55
