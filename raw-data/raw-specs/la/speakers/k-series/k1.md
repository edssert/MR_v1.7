# K1 (L-Acoustics, K Series)

## 출처 1: K1_AE_EN.docx (동일 폴더에 원본 보관)

- Product line: K1 - Long throw line source, K Series
- Long throw > 35 m, deployment in vertical lines, incremental coverage with variable inter-element angles

**Short description**
3-way active enclosure with two 15 in drivers, four 6.5 in and three 3 in diaphragm compression driver - 5-degree enclosure angle with a 90-degree waveguide directivity for assembling enclosures to achieve a coherent wavefront line source.

**Description**
3-way active enclosure with two weather-resistant premium 15 in transducers, four 6.5 in medium drivers and three high-output 3 in diaphragm compression driver coupled to a cylindrical waveguide with 90 degrees of horizontal directivity and 5 degrees of vertical coverage. The enclosure cabinet is constructed of premium grade Baltic beech and birch plywood. The enclosure front is protected by a coated steel grill and an acoustically transparent fabric. The enclosure integrates four ergonomic handles. A four-point flush-fitting hardware for rigging and combining with other loudspeakers of the same family to achieve a coherent wavefront line source. The line source directivity depends upon the number of elements and inter-element angles and can be matched to the audience geometry. Exclusively driven and protected by a proprietary amplified controller through a dedicated preset.

**Technical requirements**

- Coverage (-6 dB):
  - Enclosure directivity: dependent upon the number of elements and the line source curvature
  - Waveguide directivity: 90° symmetric
- Transducers:
  - LF transducer: 2 × 15" cone drivers
  - MF transducer: 4 × 6.5" cone drivers
  - HF transducer: 3 × 3" diaphragm compression driver
- Acoustics:
  - Nominal impedance: LF 2 × 8 Ω / MF 8 Ω / HF 8 Ω
  - Usable bandwidth: 35 Hz - 20 kHz (-10 dB), 38 Hz - 19 kHz (-6 dB), 42 Hz - 19 kHz (-3 dB)
  - RMS power handling (calculated using the mean impedance measured on the usable bandwidth): LF 422 W / MF 497 W / HF 199 W
  - Maximum SPL: 149 dB (peak level measured at 1 m under free field conditions using pink noise with crest factor 4)

**Physical data**

- Acoustical load: bass-reflex
- Cabinet: premium grade Baltic birch plywood
- Dimensions (W, H, D): 1342 mm, 438 mm, 520 mm / 52.8 in, 17.2 in, 20.5 in
- Weight (net): 106 kg / 234 lb
- Connectors: IN 1 × 8-point PA-COM / LINK 1 × 8-point PA-COM
- IP: IP43
- Finish: grey brown RAL 8019
