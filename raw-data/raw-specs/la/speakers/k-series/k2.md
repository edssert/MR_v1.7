**Short description**
3-way active enclosure with two 12 in drivers, four 6.5 in and two 3 in diaphragm compression driver - 10-degree enclosure angle with adjustable waveguide directivity for assembling enclosures to achieve a coherent wavefront line source.

**Description**
3-way active enclosure with two weather-resistant premium 12 in transducers, four 6.5 in medium drivers and two high-output 3 in diaphragm compression driver coupled to a cylindrical waveguide with user-adjustable horizontal directivity and 10 degrees of vertical coverage. The enclosure cabinet is constructed of premium grade Baltic beech and birch plywood. The enclosure front is protected by a coated steel grill and an acoustically transparent fabric. The enclosure integrates two ergonomic handles. A four-point flush-fitting hardware allows for rigging and combining with other loudspeakers of the same family to achieve a coherent wavefront line source. The line source directivity depends upon the number of elements and inter-element angles and can be matched to the audience geometry. Exclusively driven and protected by a proprietary amplified controller through a dedicated preset.

**Technical requirements**

- Coverage:
  - Enclosure directivity: dependent upon the number of elements and the line source curvature
  - Waveguide directivity: 110°/70° symmetric or 90° asymmetric (35°/55° or 55°/35°)
- Transducers:
  - LF transducer: 2 × 12" cone drivers
  - MF transducer: 4 × 6.5" cone drivers
  - HF transducer: 2 × 3" diaphragm compression driver
- Acoustics:
  - Nominal impedance: LF 2 × 8 Ω / MF 8 Ω / HF 16 Ω
  - Usable bandwidth: 35 Hz - 20 kHz (-10 dB), 38 Hz - 20 kHz (-6 dB), 40 Hz - 20 kHz (-3 dB)
  - RMS power handling (calculated using the mean impedance measured on the usable bandwidth): LF 311 W / MF 324 W / HF 117 W
  - Maximum SPL: 147 dB (peak level measured at 1 m under free field conditions using pink noise with crest factor 4)
